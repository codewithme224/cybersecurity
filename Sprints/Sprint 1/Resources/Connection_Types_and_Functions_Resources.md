# Resources: Connection Types and Their Functions

## 📚 Reading & Deep Dives
- [USB Rubber Ducky Official Site](https://hak5.org/products/usb-rubber-ducky) - The gold standard for understanding HID attacks. Read their "how it works" section.
- [Thunderclap: Attacking Thunderbolt](https://arxiv.org/abs/2002.00000) - A technical paper on DMA attacks (Advanced).
- [The 802.1X Standard](https://www.cisco.com/c/en/us/support/docs/lan-switching/port-security/102242-port-security-overview.html) - Understanding how enterprises lock down physical Ethernet ports.

## 🛠️ Tools & Hardware
- **USB Data Blocker ("USB Condom"):** A physical adapter that disconnects the data pins of a USB cable, allowing only power to pass.
- **USB Proxy/Sniffer:** Hardware that allows you to see exactly what data a USB device is sending to the host.

## 📺 Recommended Viewing
- Search YouTube for **"BadUSB explained"** to see a live demonstration of an HID attack.
- Search for **"How DMA attacks work"** to visualize the bypass of the CPU.
