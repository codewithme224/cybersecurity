# Resources: Computer Hardware Components

## 📚 Reading & Guides
- [Computer Hardware Basics (Crucial)](https://www.crucial.com/articles/pc-builders/how-to-build-a-computer) - A great guide on what each part actually does.
- [The OSI Model - Layer 1 (Physical)](https://www.cloudflare.com/learning/network-layer/what-is-the-physical-layer/) - Understanding how hardware fits into the networking model.
- [UEFI vs BIOS](https://www.computerhope.com/jargon/u/uefi.htm) - Deep dive into the firmware that starts your system.

## 🛠️ Tools for Exploration
- **CPU-Z**: A free tool to inspect your own CPU, RAM, and Motherboard specs in real-time.
- **HWMonitor**: Monitor hardware temperatures and voltages to understand the physical constraints of hardware.
- **Wireshark**: (For later) Use this to see how your NIC handles packets.
